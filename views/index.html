A Little About Me
Hello! My name is Erika Yardumian and I'm a full-stack web-developer, driven to build beautiful web applications. My journey started when I was a K-12 teacher and began to develop websites for my classes. After a few years, it became apparent that my passion for web development surpassed my love for teaching. I began to formalize my education in coding by completing a full-stack web development bootcamp at UCI and am currently attending Auburn University to aquire a BS in Computer Science. I enjoy the challenges of solving the complex problems that coding entails and the satisfaction of producing a polished, functional website. When I'm not coding, you can find me hanging out at the beach with my family or snowboarding down the slopes of Mammoth.
